Grid

This repsonsive grid was created to in order to give equal divisions across the content area throughout the site. We have created a 6, 5, and 4 column grid. This provides a variety of different options for layout and design as long as the amount of columns equals to the span of the grid. The margin is equal to 30px for each column. For mobile phones the columns will stack vertically.

This grid was generated at responsivegridsystem.com, using a margin of 3.49% which is equal to 30px, the amount of padding used for the gutters on the main content area of grad.ucla.edu. The grid is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 3.0 License.

Color Palette

University of California, Los Angeles

Color is an important element of brand identity. Like the iconic red of Coca-Cola® and the unmistakable brown of UPS®, color helps your audience identify who you are at a glance. Blue and gold are the brand colors for every University of California campus, with variation between schools. The UCLA color palette honors this, but our variation includes “hero” colors, the vivid blue and gold hues worthy of optimists. There are primary, secondary and tertiary palettes. Correct usage of these palettes in print or digital forms is vital to upholding the visual legacy of the UCLA brand.

Toggle Swtich


The toggle swtich is designed to turn on/off settings or parameters. The state of the toggle is determined by the color. Gray is off or disabled while blue is on or active.
